Multinational Company

KTM AG is an Austrian motorcycle, bicycle and sports car manufacturer owned by Pierer Mobility AG and the Indian manufacturer Bajaj Auto. It was formed in 1992 but traces its foundation to as early as 1934. Today, KTM AG is the parent company of the KTM Group, consisting of a number of motorcycle brands.

KTM bikes are the most competitive ones worldwide. The company is owned by two leading manufacturers of powered two-wheelers, Bajaj Auto and Pierer Mobility AG. It produces an impressive number of motorcycles and is one of the top motorcycle manufacturers in Europe. In addition, there is a wide range of motorised vehicles designed exclusively for recreational purposes.
